⚡ Solar ATAP 全新计费机制 ⚡ Solar ATAP New Billing System

💡 什么是 Solar ATAP？
💡 What is Solar ATAP?

自 2026 年 1 月 1 日起，大马政府正式启动全新的 Solar ATAP 框架，全面取代了旧的 NEM 3.0 系统。

Starting January 1, 2026, Malaysia's government launched the new Solar ATAP framework, completely replacing the old NEM 3.0 system.

全称：Solar Accelerated Transition Action Programme

Full Name: Solar Accelerated Transition Action Programme

它是大马政府（通过永续能源发展机构 SEDA）制定的最新政策，规定了您的屋顶太阳能如何与国能（TNB）电网连���，以及您多发的电如何用来扣除电费。

It is Malaysia's latest policy (via SEDA) that defines how your rooftop solar connects to the TNB grid and how excess power reduces your electricity bill.

✅ 新机制优势 ✅ New System Advantages

不再设"固定总配额"限制，让更多住宅和企业能更自由地安装太阳能、节省电费！

No more total quota limits, allowing more homes and businesses to freely install solar and save on electricity!

⚙️ 它是如何帮您省钱的？（运作原理）
⚙️ How Does It Save You Money? (Operating Principle)

ATAP 采用"自用优先，多余回扣"的精明计费模式：

ATAP uses a smart "self-use first, excess credit" billing model:

  • 白天直接免费用：太阳能板发电后，直接供您家里的冷气、冰箱等电器使用。这部分电完全免费，帮您省下高昂的 TNB 电费。
  • Free Daytime Use: Solar panels directly power your air conditioning, refrigerator, etc. This electricity is completely free, saving you high TNB bills.
  • 多余电量卖给 TNB：如果您家里没人，用不完的电会自动输送回 TNB 电网。
  • Sell Excess to TNB: If no one's home, unused power automatically feeds back to the TNB grid.
  • 电费单直接扣除：输送回电网的电量会转化为电费信用（Credits），直接在您当月的 TNB 电费单里扣除。（住宅用户直接抵扣每度电的零售电价成本）。
  • Direct Bill Credit: Power fed back becomes electricity credits, deducted directly from your current TNB bill (residential users get retail rate credits).
📋 住宅用户的安装容量限制
📋 Residential Installation Capacity Limits

根据您的电表类型，新政策给予了非常充足的安装额度：

Based on your meter type, the new policy provides generous installation limits:

  • 单相电表（Single-Phase）：最高可安装 5 kW
  • Single-Phase Meter: Maximum 5 kW installation
  • 三相电表（Three-Phase）：最高可安装 15 kW
  • Three-Phase Meter: Maximum 15 kW installation
  • （一般家庭绰绰有余）
  • (More than enough for typical homes)
⚠️ 客户必须注意的重要规则
⚠️ Important Rules to Remember
  • 每月结算，不滚存：太阳能回扣是在当月电费单中直接扣除。如果发出的电太多，把当月电费扣到 RM0 还有剩余，剩余的额度不能带到下个月，也不会换成现金发放。
  • Monthly Settlement, No Rollover: Solar credits are deducted from your current bill. If you have excess credits after paying to RM0, they cannot carry over to next month or be paid in cash.
  • 因此：我们会为您量身定制最精准的系统容量，绝不让您花冤枉钱多装。
  • Therefore: We custom-design your system perfectly so you don't overspend on excess capacity.
  • 10年合约期：与 TNB 签署的 ATAP 发电回扣合约期限为 10 年。10年之后，系统依然属于您并可以继续免费发电供家里自用。
  • 10-Year Contract: Your ATAP electricity credit contract with TNB is for 10 years. After 10 years, the system is still yours and continues generating free power for your home.
💡 Right-Sizing 重要性 💡 Right-Sizing Importance

在新的 ATAP 机制下，太阳能系统并非"越大越好"，而是需要根据您家庭白天的实际用电比例进行精准量身设计，才能让投资回报率（ROI）最大化。

Under the new ATAP system, bigger isn't always better. The system should be precisely designed based on your home's actual daytime power usage to maximize ROI.
